# NNNN. Short imperative title

- **Status:** Proposed | Accepted | Superseded by [NNNN](NNNN-...md)
- **Date:** YYYY-MM-DD

## Context

What is the situation that forced this decision? What constraints are at play (technical, time, cost, knowledge)? One or two paragraphs — enough that a future reader without today's context can follow.

## Decision

The choice, stated plainly. One paragraph. If it's a multi-part decision, use a short bulleted list.

## Alternatives considered

- **Option A** — why rejected.
- **Option B** — why rejected.

Be honest about runners-up. "Nothing else was viable" is rarely true and rarely useful to a future reader.

## Consequences

What changes because of this decision? Include both the good (what it unlocks) and the bad (what it makes harder or forecloses). If there's an exit ramp — conditions under which we'd revisit — name it.
